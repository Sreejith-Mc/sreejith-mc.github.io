01 · about-me

Design that survives contact with engineering.

auto layout · 3 components

I'm Alex — a UI/UX designer who treats every product like a design system waiting to happen. For five years I've helped startups and enterprise teams turn vague briefs into shippable, scalable interfaces without losing the craft.

My happy place is the gap between design and engineering: tidy components, named layers, tokens that map one-to-one to code, and prototypes that answer questions before sprint planning does. If a developer has ever smiled at your handoff file, there's a decent chance I made it.
